# Butterfly Labs AI Research

**Butterfly Labs** is a nonprofit shaping a human-centered AI future through storytelling, education, and research.  
We study how artificial intelligence can be made transparent, ethical, and beneficial for everyone.

## Focus Areas
- Responsible & Explainable AI
- LLM Evaluation & Alignment
- AI Literacy & Public Awareness
- Creative and Educational AI Applications
